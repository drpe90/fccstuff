H. P. Lovecraft Select Bibliography

"The oldest and strongest emotion of mankind is fear. And the oldest and strongest kind of fear is the fear of the unknown."

- H. P. Lovecraft


These are some books worth reading, if you are into horror/mystery genre:
  • "Dagon" (1917)
  • "The Doom that Came to Sarnath" (1919)
  • "From Beyond" (1920)
  • "Nyarlathotep" (1920)
  • "The Nameless City" (1921)
  • "Azathoth" (1922)
  • "The Lurking Fear" (1922)
  • "The Call of Cthulhu" (1926)
  • "The Dunwich Horror" (1928)
  • "The Whisperer in Darkness" (1930)
  • "At the Mountains of Madness" (1931)
  • "The Shadow over Innsmouth" (1931)
For a full list of Lovecraft's books, please visit this Wiki page.
